Athletics

The school-wide athletics day is held early in term one. All events are held on the school fields on the day (with the exception of qualifying heats for some events which are held in the week leading up to athletics day).

Here are some of the events at athletics:

Running:

  • 100m
  • 200m
  • 300m
  • 400m
  • 800m
  • 1500m
  • 3000m
  • Hurdles

Jumping:

  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • Triple Jump

Throwing:

  • Discus
  • Javelin
  • Shotput

Staff of the school run the events and score keeping and records are kept each year not only of the winning whānau (the most points across all events) but also of the best individual performances of the year and the all-time school record holders for the events.
