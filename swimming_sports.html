Swimming Sports

Swimming sports also happens during term one, though unlike athletics day it is not compulsory for all students to attend. The school does not have a swimming pool so competing students travel to a public pool in Papatoetoe to compete. Like athletics day there are competitive and non-competitive events and students can gain points for their whānau through participation in the non-competitive events or through finishing well in the competitive events.

Here are some of the events at swimming sports:

  • Backstroke
  • Breaststroke
  • Butterfly
  • Freestyle
  • Individual medley
  • Whānau relay
